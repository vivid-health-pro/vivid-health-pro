Beta Tester Guide — Thank you for helping!

🔒 Early Access · Free for Testers
1

Check your Gmail account on your Android phone

The email I sent your test invitation to must be the same Gmail account logged into your Android phone and Google Play Store.

To check which account is active on Play Store:

  • Open Google Play Store app
  • Tap your profile picture (top right)
  • Check which Gmail account is shown
  • It must match the email I added as a tester
💡 If you have multiple Google accounts on your phone, make sure the correct one is selected in Play Store.
2

Open the invitation email I sent you

I sent you an email with the subject "You're invited to test Vivid Health Pro"

  • Check your Inbox first
  • If not there, check your Spam / Junk folder
  • Mark it as "Not Spam" if found in spam
⚠️ Check your spam folder! Google Play tester invitations sometimes go to spam.
3

Click the opt-in link in the email

In the email, click the link that says "Become a tester" or the opt-in URL.

  • It will open a Google Play page
  • Tap "Become a tester" button
  • Then tap "Download it on Google Play"
💡 Do this on your Android phone, not on a computer.
4

Install the app from Google Play

Google Play will open showing Vivid Health Pro (Early Access)

  • Tap Install (it's free for testers)
  • Wait for download to complete (~3MB)
  • Tap Open
5

Create your account in the app

When the app opens you'll see a sign in screen:

  • Tap "Create account" tab
  • Enter your name
  • Enter your Gmail address (same one used above)
  • Create a password — must have uppercase, lowercase, number and symbol (e.g. Health123!)
  • Tap "Create account →"
⚠️ A 6-digit verification code will be sent to your email. Check your spam folder if you don't see it!
6

Verify your email

  • Check your email for a 6-digit code from no-reply@verificationemail.com
  • Check spam folder if not in inbox
  • Enter the 6-digit code in the app
  • Tap "Verify email →"
  • Then tap "Sign in" and log in with your email and password
7

Complete your profile setup

The app will walk you through a quick setup:

  • Enter your name, age, gender
  • Enter your height and weight
  • Select your activity level
  • Select your diet preference
  • Set your health goal
  • Tap Finish
